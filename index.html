Our Menu

Chicken

Savor the symphony of flavors in our Herb-Crusted Roasted Chicken. This dish begins with a tender, free-range chicken, meticulously marinated in a blend of aromatic herbs and spices. Slow-roasted to perfection, the chicken boasts a golden, crisp exterior, while preserving a succulently moist interior. Accompanied by a hearty serving of garlic Parmesan potatoes, each bite offers a creamy texture with a hint of sharp, nutty Parmesan and a burst of garlicky goodness. Drizzled with a homemade rosemary-infused gravy, this dish promises a delightful harmony of rustic flavors. Perfect for a cozy dinner or a special occasion, this entrée is a true testament to classic culinary artistry.

Beef

Indulge in the luxurious flavors of our Char-Grilled Wagyu Beef. This exquisite dish features premium, marbled Wagyu beef, known for its extraordinary tenderness and depth of flavor. Expertly grilled to your preferred doneness, the beef is infused with the subtle, earthy notes of truffle, elevating its natural richness. Served with a velvety truffle-infused demi-glace that perfectly complements the meat’s juicy succulence, and accompanied by a side of delicately roasted seasonal vegetables. This dish is a true culinary masterpiece, crafted to satisfy the palates of the most discerning gourmets.

Sushi

Experience the art of Japanese cuisine with our Signature Omakase Sushi Platter. 'Omakase' – meaning 'I leave it up to you' – is a culinary journey where the chef selects the freshest, most delectable seafood of the day. Our sushi masters meticulously craft each piece, showcasing the delicate flavors of tuna, salmon, yellowtail, and other seasonal catches. Paired with perfectly seasoned sushi rice and garnished with hints of wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger, this assortment offers a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Each bite is not just a meal but a story of tradition and innovation, promising an unforgettable dining experience.
